My Mission-The 5 Cs

  1. Communication
  2. Creativity
  3. Collaboration
  4. Critical Thinking
  5. Confidence

Jim Palmarini of the National Federation of State High School Association (NFHS) identifies 4 Cs associated with theater: communication, creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking. The four Cs teach participants critical skills that will benefit them throughout their lives. During my theater experience, I subconsciously applied the four Cs. Now I am an advocate for these four Cs and even created a fifth C: confidence. I have been blessed with opportunities to implement the five Cs not only in my life, but in the lives of young performers.
